These functions are part of a module downloaded from DropBox. These and other functions within the [module](https://patrickjhess.github.io/Imported-Functions/intro.html#functions-imported-by-the-volume-basic-concepts-of-fixed-income) are intended to shift focus from coding to financial concepts.
